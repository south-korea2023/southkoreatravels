South Korea is home to one of the world’s most diverse and acquired cuisines that not only warms the hearts of locals and travelers alike but also fill their stomachs. Even though Seoul showcases many globalized delicacies, the traditional favorites are loved the most

Whether you’re looking for a quick, salty snack or an adventure for your taste buds, you're in right place to find out about some of the best Korean street food!

  1. Savory Foods
    2.
    • Fried snacks (튀김 | twigim)

      •

      Yep, it’s exactly what it sounds like! Everybody loves to eat Korean fried food.

    • Rice cake skewers (떡꼬치 | tteokkkochi)



      • Sometimes simple is best, and the insanely popular spicy rice cakes go to show it! Taste and try Korean rice cake skewers

    • Potato Hot Dogs (감자핫도그 | gamjahatdogeu)



      • This food is literally a hot dog cooked to perfection and then covered in crispy, golden potatoes. What’s not to love? Pick up a potato-covered hot dog

  2. Deserts

    • Ice cream-filled Korean waffles (아이스크림 와플)

      •

      This one almost doesn’t need an explanation – who doesn’t love ice cream or waffles? This is one of the popular Korean street foods in Seoul.

    • Sugar Lollipop (설탕뽑기 | seoltangppopgi)

      •

      Korean lollipops are street food that is right up your alley.You might find this familiar if you have watched "Squid Game" !!

    • Korean Fish Bread (붕어빵 | bungeoppang)

      •

      The warmth combined with the crunch of the freshly toasted bread and the sweetness of the red bean paste filling makes for a delicious and easy-to-eat dessert treat.
